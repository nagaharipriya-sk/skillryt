About

Bill Gates is an American business magnate, software developer, investor, author, and philanthropist. He is best known as the co-founder of Microsoft, one of the world’s largest and most influential technology companies. Born on October 28, 1955, he showed an early interest in computer programming and developed software during his school days. In 1975, along with his childhood friend Paul Allen, Bill Gates founded Microsoft with a vision to put a computer on every desk and in every home. Under his leadership, Microsoft became the global leader in personal computer software, especially with the success of the Windows operating system. After stepping down from his full-time role at Microsoft, Bill Gates shifted his primary focus to philanthropy. He is the co-founder of the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, one of the largest private charitable organizations in the world. The foundation works in the areas of global health, education, poverty reduction, and disease prevention. Bill Gates is widely respected not only for his contribution to technology but also for his commitment to improving lives across the globe through innovation and charity.

Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation

Bill Gates is the co-founder of the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, one of the largest private charitable organizations in the world. The foundation was established in the year 2000 with the mission to improve global health, reduce extreme poverty, and expand access to education and technology. The foundation works across many countries to fight deadly diseases such as malaria, tuberculosis, and polio. It also supports vaccination programs, clean drinking water projects, agricultural development, and quality education for underprivileged children. Through this foundation, Bill Gates has donated billions of dollars to improve the lives of people worldwide. Today, the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation plays a major role in global development and humanitarian efforts, making Bill Gates one of the world’s most impactful philanthropists.
