Flowers are the colorful, reproductive parts of flowering plants, also known as blooms or blossoms. They play crucial roles in ecosystems by attracting pollinators like bees and birds for reproduction and providing food in the form of nectar and pollen. They are also used by humans for decoration, in perfumes, medicine, and as gifts. Examples include roses, daisies, and tulips, which come in many different shapes, sizes, and colors.
